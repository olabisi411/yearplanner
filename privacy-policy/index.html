Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 31, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Brandnosis ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in the Year Planner mobile application (the "App").

1. Data Collection and Usage

User-Generated Data: All data you enter regarding your goals, vision, and progress is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to our servers.

Automatically Collected Data: To provide and maintain the service, including the display of advertisements, certain information is collected automatically by third-party services.

2. Advertising and Third-Party Services

The App includes third-party advertising services (such as Google AdMob) to remain free to use. These third-party providers may collect and use certain data to show ads that are relevant to you. This data may include:

  • IP Address
  • Advertising ID (AAID on Android)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Interaction data with ads shown in the App

These third parties have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the policy of our primary ad provider:

Google AdMob & AdSense: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Permissions

The App may require the following permissions:

  • Internet Access: To load advertisements and sync app settings.
  • Access Network State: To check if a connection is available for ads.
  • Advertising ID Permission: To provide personalized advertising.

4. Your Choices (Opt-Out)

You can reset your advertising identifier or opt-out of personalized ads through your Android device settings:

Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization (or "Delete Advertising ID").

Your planning data is managed locally. Uninstalling the app will delete all local data. Data collected by advertising partners is subject to their respective retention policies.
